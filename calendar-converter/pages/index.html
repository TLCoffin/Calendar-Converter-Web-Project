Veridical Solar Calendar

The Veridical Solar Calendar (VSC) was created by two high school students Luke Bushuk and Verne Wright in 2024 designed to improve upon the Gregorian calendars flaws, by introducing 13 uniform months each having 28 days. An extra "New Years Day" would be added as well as a "Leap Day" on leap years, to steer the yearly drift back on course.

Importance of VSC

The months of VSC are named after discovered moons in the Solar System.
